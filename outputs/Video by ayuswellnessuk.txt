If your organs could speak, here's what they would ask you to eat. Your heart would ask for tomatoes. Packed with lycopene, tomatoes help lower LDL cholesterol and protect against free radical damage, reducing the risk of heart disease. Your kidneys would ask for water and red fruits. Blueberries and cranberries are rich in antioxidants and nutrients that cleanse the body and protect the kidneys from infections. Your brain would ask for nuts. Loaded with omega-3 fatty acids, nuts support brain function, improve memory and help prevent cognitive decline. Your liver would ask for green tea. High in catechins, green tea enhances liver function and protects against diseases like fatty liver. Your uterus would ask for avocado. Avocados provide folic acid, potassium and vitamin E.
